💡 How AI tokens work

Each AI chart generation and insight uses tokens from your monthly budget. Plus gets 200,000 tokens/month (~200 chart generations) and Pro gets 2,000,000 tokens/month (~2,000 chart generations). Unused tokens don't roll over. Your budget resets on the 1st of each month. You can always see your usage in Settings → AI Usage.
