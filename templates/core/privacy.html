Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025  ·  Effective: April 15, 2025

Nexyza ("we", "us", "our") is a data analytics platform operated by Nexyza (nexyza.com). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect your email address and, optionally, your first and last name. We use these to identify your account, send transactional emails (password resets, invoice receipts), and communicate service updates.

1.2 Files You Upload

You may upload CSV, Excel, or JSON files to Nexyza for analysis. These files are stored on our servers solely to provide the analysis service to you. They are processed in isolation — your files are never visible to other users unless you deliberately create and share a share link. You can permanently delete any uploaded file from your dashboard at any time.

1.3 Usage Data

We collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser user agent strings, pages visited, and timestamps. This data is used for security monitoring, debugging, and aggregate analytics (e.g. understanding which features are most used). We do not sell this data.

1.4 Billing Information

Payments are processed by LemonSqueezy, a third-party payment processor. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details on our servers. We receive a subscription record and customer identifier from LemonSqueezy for account management purposes. LemonSqueezy's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.

1.5 AI Queries

When you use AI features (chart generation, natural language queries, anomaly narratives), your query and a summary of the relevant data columns are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. We do not use your data to train AI models. Anthropic's usage policy governs how they handle API inputs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery — to run your analyses, generate charts, store results, and provide all features of the platform.
  • Communication — to send password reset emails, subscription receipts, scheduled report emails, and important service notices. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit opt-in.
  • Security — to detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorised access.
  • Improvement — to understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the product. This analysis is done on anonymised or aggregated data.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — we use Anthropic (AI), LemonSqueezy (payments), and Hostinger (email/hosting) to operate the service. Each is bound by their own data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Nexyza, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers — if Nexyza is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email before any such transfer.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data and uploaded files for as long as your account is active. If you delete a file from your dashboard, it is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain certain records (e.g. billing records for tax purposes, which are kept for 7 years).

5. Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Uploaded files are stored with server-side encryption. We implement access controls, audit logging, and monitoring to protect against unauthorised access. No security system is perfect — if you discover a vulnerability, please contact us at hello@nexyza.com.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — update inaccurate information via your account settings.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Danger Zone.
  • Portability — export your analysis results as CSV, Excel, JSON, or PDF at any time.
  • Objection — contact us to object to any processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@nexyza.com with the subject line "Privacy Request".

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies necessary to operate the service: a session cookie to keep you logged in, and a CSRF token cookie for security. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

8. Children's Privacy

Nexyza is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, for significant changes, notify users by email at least 14 days before the change takes effect.
